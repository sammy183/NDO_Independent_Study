OVERVIEW:

Data from: https://m-selig.ae.illinois.edu/props/propDB.html
Many thanks to John Brandt, Robert Deters, Gavin Ananda, Or Dantsker, and Michael Selig for gathering and publishing this data

the files are named:
ManufacturerType_Diameter(in)xPitch(in)_#Tester_RPM

i.e. ance_8.5x6_2849cm_4000 refers to the aeronaut carbon electric 8.5x6 prop in test #2849 by Craig McGrath (cm) at 4000 RPM
